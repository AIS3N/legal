# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 2026

## What SmartLayout does

SmartLayout reads your Screen Time usage data to rank your apps by frequency and generate a suggested home screen layout.

## Data collection

**SmartLayout collects no data.**

- All analysis runs entirely on your device using Apple's DeviceActivity framework.
- No usage data, app names, identifiers, or personal information is ever transmitted, stored on a server, or shared with any third party.
- SmartLayout has no backend, no analytics, and no account system.

## Screen Time permission

SmartLayout requests access to Screen Time data via Apple's FamilyControls framework. This permission is used solely to rank your apps by usage. You can revoke it at any time in **Settings > Screen Time**.

## In-app purchase

SmartLayout offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full layout. Purchase records are managed by Apple's StoreKit and are not accessible to us beyond confirming whether a purchase was made.
